Sales workflow optimization tool.

Sometimes internal systems start getting obsolete and every time someone new arrives at the company, the process of incorporation is complicated and expensive.

For Ferreterías Oviedo® we developed a client/server webapp that 10X the quotation generation process.

The app presents an easy-to-use interface that hides all the complexities associated with having multiple sources of data and separated systems to access them. To achive this, in the back-end, we built a customized search engine on top of their product databases & third party catalogue and a quoting system that generates beautiful PDF documents to send to clients.
